Passport Index: A Look at a Country's Freedom of Travel

Using the Henley Passport Index, we can take a look at the travel freedom of countries around the world. For each country it complies the number of possible destinations that it's passport holders can travel without requiring a visa.


Most countries have a low number of countries that they can access with over 50 countries being able to enter less than 60 countries.

Singapore and Canada appears on multiple positions of the top 10 rankings.
